Hi there! My name is James Carnahan, and I've worked as a ride operator at LEGOLAND California Resort in Carlsbad. My job entailed helping people get to where they needed to go in the park, as well as helping lines at rides move smoothly. I got the opportunity to help at many rides around the park, and at each one I was privileged to assist in making childrens' experiences great. I got to ride with them when they didn't have a chaperone who was able, and saw happy faces that ensued, which made the long hours standing around worth it.

I've also volunteered at the Shultz Museum in Santa Rosa, California. I did this over the summer after my 7th grade year. Working with children was a joy; I was able to see their curiosity while they made movies and learned how to draw like Charles Shultz.

I used to be in Cub Scouts when I was younger. There was many a time where we had to raise funds for our organization, and one of those examples is when I helped manage a Dime Toss booth at the fair in Santa Rosa. While there, I was able to develop my professional social skills, and learn how to successfully interact with others. Like my job at LEGOLAND, we worked for hours in the hot sun, enduring for a common goal.
